Ecological Imbalance

Ecological Imbalance is when something disrupts the balance of the ecosystem. It could be natural or human-caused. As you know there is a balance in nature about everything. The slightest changes could cause a lot of damage. For example, if tigers weren't there then the deer population would increase and because of the excess of deers they would eat too many plants and the plant population would decrease. The main causes of it are degradation of land and soil erosion, deforestation, faulty utilisation of water resources, environmental problems from faulty mining practices, industrial and atmospheric pollution. There are some ways we can prevent ecological imblance, they are, we can stop hunting, we can elliminate pollution from its source and we can avoid deforestation.
